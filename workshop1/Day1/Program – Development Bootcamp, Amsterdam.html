Three Levels

Choose Yours!

Beginner Bootcamp

12-18 July, 2015 and other dates..

This will teach you the basics of programming, and how to think like a programmer. By the end of the week, you will be able to start writing small programs to make your live easier and you will have built your first interactive website.

Intermediate Bootcamp

22-28 November, 2015 and other dates..

Your code will come alive on the internet, where the world will be able to see what you built. An exciting week, after which you will have built a couple of applications, and released them to the public internet.

Advanced Bootcamp

20-26 March, 2016 and other dates..

This will prepare you for the professional programming world out there. You will add more complicated technology to your applications, and you will learn about the latest hi-tech developments and how to use them.

A full week primer that teaches you to build and maintain your own website or web app.

The Beginner Program

A First Taste of Code

We will cover all the basic parts of a web application. By the end of the week you will have built your own website, and you will know how to grow and maintain it.

One year of hosting is included in the program so your website will stay online for that time and you will be able to show it to your friends, family, or even start your own business maybe?
